The Underground Bluefin Tuna Trade in Thailand

Category: Food Smuggling | Country: Thailand | Crime Type: Illegal Import Ring

The Problem

Bluefin Tuna has become a target for international smuggling operations due to its high market value and strong global demand. Investigators in Thailand have uncovered shipments hidden inside legitimate cargo containers, often mislabeled to avoid customs detection. Authorities including the FDA’s Office of Criminal Investigations have documented cases where distributors knowingly imported illegal or unregulated Bluefin Tuna. These operations reveal how profitable the underground food trade has become and why enforcement agencies continue to monitor suspicious imports.

Interesting Fact

Authorities have seized shipments of this product during international inspections.
