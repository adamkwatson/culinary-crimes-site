The Underground Olive Oil Trade in China

Category: Food Fraud | Country: China | Crime Type: Mislabeling Investigation

The Problem

Olive Oil has repeatedly appeared in food fraud investigations involving counterfeit or adulterated products. In China, regulators discovered shipments labeled as premium Olive Oil that were diluted or substituted with cheaper ingredients. Such schemes allow dishonest suppliers to increase profits while deceiving consumers. Agencies including the FDA’s Office of Criminal Investigations have pursued cases involving distributors who knowingly sold fraudulent Olive Oil, highlighting the ongoing challenges in protecting the integrity of the global food supply.
