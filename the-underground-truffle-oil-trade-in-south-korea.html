The Underground Truffle Oil Trade in South Korea

Category: Food Fraud | Country: South Korea | Crime Type: Mislabeling Investigation

The Problem

Truffle Oil has repeatedly appeared in food fraud investigations involving counterfeit or adulterated products. In South Korea, regulators discovered shipments labeled as premium Truffle Oil that were diluted or substituted with cheaper ingredients. Such schemes allow dishonest suppliers to increase profits while deceiving consumers. Agencies including the FDA’s Office of Criminal Investigations have pursued cases involving distributors who knowingly sold fraudulent Truffle Oil, highlighting the ongoing challenges in protecting the integrity of the global food supply.

Interesting Fact

Experts consider this product highly vulnerable to fraud due to its market value.
