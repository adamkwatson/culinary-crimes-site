The Shark Fin Fraud Investigation in China

The Problem

Shark Fin has become a target for international smuggling operations due to its high market value and strong global demand. Investigators in China have uncovered shipments hidden inside legitimate cargo containers, often mislabeled to avoid customs detection. Authorities including the FDA’s Office of Criminal Investigations have documented cases where distributors knowingly imported illegal or unregulated Shark Fin. These operations reveal how profitable the underground food trade has become and why enforcement agencies continue to monitor suspicious imports.

Interesting Fact

Regulators continue to monitor global trade involving this ingredient.
