The Underground Beluga Caviar Trade in India

Category: Food Smuggling | Country: India | Crime Type: Underground Trade Network

The Problem

Beluga Caviar has become a target for international smuggling operations due to its high market value and strong global demand. Investigators in India have uncovered shipments hidden inside legitimate cargo containers, often mislabeled to avoid customs detection. Authorities including the FDA’s Office of Criminal Investigations have documented cases where distributors knowingly imported illegal or unregulated Beluga Caviar. These operations reveal how profitable the underground food trade has become and why enforcement agencies continue to monitor suspicious imports.

Interesting Fact

Experts consider this product highly vulnerable to fraud due to its market value.
