The Foie Gras Counterfeit Scandal in Brazil

The Problem

Foie Gras has become a target for international smuggling operations due to its high market value and strong global demand. Investigators in Brazil have uncovered shipments hidden inside legitimate cargo containers, often mislabeled to avoid customs detection. Authorities including the FDA’s Office of Criminal Investigations have documented cases where distributors knowingly imported illegal or unregulated Foie Gras. These operations reveal how profitable the underground food trade has become and why enforcement agencies continue to monitor suspicious imports.
